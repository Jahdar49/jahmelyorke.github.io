About Sodaina Boken

Sodaina Boken is a Japanese adventure game which utilizes HTML and CSS. The object of the game is choose your path by clicking on the highlighted images displayed throughout the game. Different image selections result in different experiences and interactions with your samarai. This results in a unique experience everytime you play.
