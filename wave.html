About Wave

Wave is a simple, arcade inspired game built from Java. The objective of the game is to move your character and avoid the incoming wave of colored lines on the screen. The spawn rate increases as you progress through the game and there is a boss stage periodically to keep you on your toes. You can purchase powerups at the shop to help your player survive to the end of the game. There is a normal mode as well as a hard mode if you're looking for more of a challenge.
