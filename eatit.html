About Eat-It

Eat-it is a JSON based food ordering app. JSON data is stored within the Firebase database and that information is then retrieved using Java. Create a unique user ID and a password to login to the database. You then have the option to select from an array of dishes, each varying in price. Select the items you wish to order, add them to your cart and your total will be displayed. When cashing out your order, enter your e-mail and your order details will be sent to you via Firebase.
